Privacy Policy — Document Scanner

Last updated: July 12, 2026

Summary

Document Scanner does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Data collection

We collect no data. The app has no analytics, no advertising SDKs, no user accounts, and makes no network requests to our servers (we operate none).

Your documents

Scanned documents and images are stored only in the app's private storage on your device. They are never uploaded anywhere. Deleting a document in the app, or uninstalling the app, permanently removes them.

Camera

The camera is used only while you are scanning a document, through Google's on-device document scanning service. Images are processed on your device.

Text recognition (OCR)

Text extraction runs fully on-device using Google ML Kit. No image or text is sent to any server by this app.

App lock

The optional app lock uses your device's own screen lock (fingerprint, face, PIN). The app never sees or stores your credentials.

Third-party services

The app uses Google Play Services on-device APIs (document scanner, text recognition). These run locally. Google's use of Play Services is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new date.
